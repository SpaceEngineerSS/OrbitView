## üõ†Ô∏è The HUD (Heads-Up Display)

### 1. Sidebar & Search
- Use the **Search Bar** to find satellites by name (e.g., "ISS", "Starlink-1024") or NORAD ID.
- Use **Filters** to show only specific categories like GPS, Starlink, or CubeSats.
- **Analyst Mode:** Toggle high-performance data overlays and coordinate grids.

### 2. Info Panel
When a satellite is selected, the Info Panel provides:
- **Telemetry:** Real-time altitude, velocity, and orbital period.
- **Visuals:** Toggle Orbit Paths, Ground Tracks, and Footprint (coverage area).
- **Pass Prediction:** See when the satellite will next pass over your location.

### 3. AR Mode (Mobile)
On compatible mobile devices, use the **Compass Icon** to enter AR mode. Point your phone at the sky to see where satellites are located in real-time relative to your physical orientation.
